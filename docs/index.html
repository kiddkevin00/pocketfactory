Build a tiny factory. Watch it run.

PocketFactory is a pocket-sized industrial sim inspired by Satisfactory. Mine ore from glowing nodes, smelt it into ingots, belt it through your line, assemble parts, and keep the lights on with your own coal power. All on a phone screen.

FEATURES

A real factory loop, sized for your thumb.

Every piece of Satisfactory's loop is here — just drawn in 2D, balanced for a phone, and shipped with offline save.

▲

Mine resources

Place Miners on iron, copper, and coal nodes scattered across the world. Each node has its own extraction rate.

●

Smelt & craft

Route ore into smelters, ingots into constructors, and parts into assemblers — 7 recipes across 4 building types.

⇢

Conveyor belts

Tap source, tap destination — done. Items animate along the belt and back up when storage fills.

⚡

Real power grid

Every building draws MW. Build coal generators to keep up — or watch the whole factory brown out.

⚛

Research tree

Spend stockpiled items to unlock copper, assemblers, double-speed belts, and overclocked power.

⤓

Offline save

Auto-saves every 10 seconds and when you background the app. Nothing leaves your device.

GETTING STARTED

Five taps to your first ingot.

The first-launch tutorial walks you through this — but here's the whole loop in one screen.

01

Place a Miner on a resource node

Tap Miner in the toolbar, then tap any glowing tile labeled Fe (iron), Cu (copper), or C (coal).

02

Place a Smelter and pick the recipe

Put a Smelter on an open tile beside your Miner. Tap it to pick a smelting recipe.

03

Connect them with a Belt

Tap Belt, tap the source building, then tap the destination. Items will start flowing.

04

Add a Storage at the end of the line

Storage is what counts toward your stockpile — and research requires stockpile.

05

Generate power before you brown out

Mine coal, belt it into a Coal Generator, and add 75 MW to your grid.
