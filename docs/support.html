Common questions

I lost my save

Saves are stored locally on your device. They persist as long as the app stays installed. If you delete the app, reinstall it, or wipe your device, the save is gone — there is no cloud sync today. If you encounter a bug that corrupts your save, email a screenshot of what happened and the approximate time, and I'll do what I can.

My factory browned out and nothing is running

Brownout means your total power draw exceeds total generation. Either build more coal generators (and belt coal into them), or remove buildings until draw is back below generation. The bar at the top of the screen shows your current draw vs. generation.

How do I research new tech?

Research costs stockpiled items — items that have reached a Storage building. The Storage box at the end of your production line tallies its inputs into your global stockpile, which is then visible in the top bar and spendable in the Research panel.

Will it come to Android / web / desktop?

PocketFactory is built on Expo, which targets iOS, Android, and web from a single codebase. The first public release focuses on iOS via the App Store. Android and a web build will follow once iOS is stable.

Reporting accessibility issues

Accessibility feedback is taken seriously. If something is hard to read, hit, or hear, please email with as much detail as possible — including device, OS version, and an example. I'll work with you on a fix.

Response time

I aim to reply to every email within three business days. If you haven't heard back in a week, please send a follow-up — it almost always means the original got buried, not ignored.

PocketFactory is a one-developer indie project. Your feedback genuinely shapes what gets built next. Thank you for playing.
